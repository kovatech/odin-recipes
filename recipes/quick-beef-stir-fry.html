Quick Beef Stir-Fry

Quick and easy beef stir-fry. Perfect after a busy day.

The perfect dish to prepare with leftovers, use whatever veggies you have,
some lean beef, soy sauce and in 25 minutes you'll have an irristably hearty dish!

Ingredients (mostly optional)

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1lb beef cut into strips
  • vegetables... what ya got?
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Let's cook... BOSH

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Heat the oil in a wok
  3. Add the beef and stir-fry until browned
  4. Add the veggies to the wok, cook for 2 minutes
  5. Stir in the soy sauce and sesame seeds, stir fry for a further 2 minutes
  6. Serve hot and enjoy... BAM
